Outspeeed 2.0 Game Overview: There are 4 players in the game and they are in the desert. The players find their first key that will allow them to enter the cave inside the game which has a bunch of puzzles. The door shuts behind them and they are forced to find another way out. Luckily, they have a flashlight to assist them and they use it to navigate themselves through puzzles that they need to solve as a team. They need to collect objects, find hidden codes, flip switches and more to be able to find their way out, they need to charge their flashlight and can find batteries to do so. however it only grants them 10%. The players can locate a charging station which grants them 100% of battery. if their battery gets to 0%. they lose. if they find a way out, they win. whether they lose or win, something, or someone had locked them in in the first place. Find 3 keys that will unlock different colored doors to find your way out of the cave. To Slow? Well you can hoverboard your way there! also while Dashing, Super Dashing, Double Jumping and Sliding through obstacles and puzzles along the way! Play on March 31st only on Roblox.
